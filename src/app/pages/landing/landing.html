Quick Reference Guide

Welcome to Thomson Digital Uniproof, the online collaborative platform tailor made for authoring, copyediting and proofing journal content.

Please follow the instructions below:

  • Review your proof carefully and make necessary amendments directly into the article.
  • Resolve (reply or accept) all Author Queries (AQs) displayed in the panel on the right-hand side. Press “Reply” to provide information if requested. Press “Accept” if the query simply requires acknowledgement.
  • You can edit author and affiliation information, tables, equations, citations, references and replace images in Thomson Digital Uniproof. Add a comment if you are unable to make a certain change.
  • The article will be auto-saved every minute or can be saved manually by pressing the Save button at the top-right-hand side of the screen. You can close the link and reopen to continue proofing if required.
  • The PDF proof will not reflect the corrections you enter in Thomson Digital Uniproof and is for reference only. If you wish to export a record of the changes you have made, press the Record of Changes button; this will prompt you to download an HTML file with changes tracked, but is not an updated proof.
  • To provide a general comment to the editor, insert a comment and select "To Editor" from the drop-down options underneath.
  • Submit the article when you have reviewed the entire article and resolved all the Author Queries.
  • When you Submit your article, you will be asked if you would like to download the Record of Changes; press Yes to download this file, or No to proceed with submission.
  • If you need assistance with Thomson Digital, please contact our support team at product.support@thomsondigital.com
  • For any other queries relating to your article, contact the journal mailbox.
  • If you are not able to use Thomson Digital to make your corrections, please annotate the PDF proof and upload it using the Upload Files option from the Home panel on the left-hand side or main toolbar at the top. You should then answer the queries and Submit your article.
  • Note that some browser add-ons can impact on the performance of Thomson Digital. If you are having problems, disable any add-ons to see if that helps.
  • You can access the Quick Reference Guide and User manual from the Help menu in the top-right corner of the screen.
